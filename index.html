The Literary Matters At Texas A&M

A graduate degree in English at Texas A&M is about more than just reading books. It's about using cutting edge methods to answer big questions about the role of the literary in shaping our past and constructing our future. We work in variety of field on a range of topics with an eye toward public engagement and practical applications.

How We Work

We work in a variety of ways, but we are strong in the following innovative methods:

  • Digital Humanities
  • Public Humanities
  • Book History
  • Feminist and Queer Cultural Studies

What We Do

We support a full range of periods, however, these are areas we are particularly strong in:

  • Early Modern & Medieval Studies
  • Childhood Studies
  • Race and Gender Studies
  • Health Humanities & Rhetoric of Science
